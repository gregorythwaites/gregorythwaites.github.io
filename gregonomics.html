Gregonomics

Advisory, speaking and bespoke economic research.

Through Gregonomics, I offer independent economic advice drawing on 25 years of experience spanning the Bank of England, academic research, international development and UK economic policy. I work with organisations that need rigorous, evidence-based analysis of the macroeconomy and labour markets.

Services

Advisory

Strategic advice on macroeconomic trends, labour market dynamics, monetary policy and structural change for businesses, investors and policymakers.

Speaking

Keynotes and panel contributions on the UK economy, AI and labour markets, inflation, productivity and the outlook for growth. Accessible to non-specialist audiences.

Bespoke Research

Custom economic analysis and data work. Expertise in firm-level microdata, survey design, causal inference and policy evaluation.

Technical Assistance

Capacity building for central banks, finance ministries and international organisations. Experience working with the IMF, UN and the Bank of England's CCBS programme.

Experience

  • 2020–
    Research Director, Resolution Foundation

    Leading the think tank's growth and labour markets research programmes. Co-led the Economy 2030 Inquiry with LSE.

  • 2020–
    Associate Professor, University of Nottingham

    Publishing peer-reviewed research, teaching the MSc course Monetary Theory and Practice, and co-directing the Decision Maker Panel with Nick Bloom (Stanford) and the Bank of England.

  • 2018–2019
    Research Director, WorldRemit

    Research Director at a leading fintech, working on international remittances and financial inclusion.

  • 2001–2018
    Bank of England

    Monetary Analysis, Financial Stability and International directorates. Head of International Research. Wrote speeches, forecasted the UK economy, managed teams of economists and represented the Bank in international fora.

  • 2010–2011
    Independent Commission on Banking (Vickers Commission)

    Seconded to the ICB, the government commission that recommended ring-fencing of UK retail banking.

  • 2005–2007
    UN Mission in Kosovo (UNMIK)

    Helping to run economic policy during Kosovo's transition to independence. Fiscal policy, budget preparation and establishment of economic institutions.

  • Various
    International consulting and technical assistance

    Consulting to the Kosovo Ministry of Finance and Central Bank of Kosovo. Technical assistance through the Bank of England's Centre for Central Banking Studies to Ukraine and central banks in low- and middle-income countries.
